MAE3 Robot Project

Description:

The goal of this project was to build a robot that could collect blocks from around the playing field and deposit them into a "goal" area to score points.

The CAD model on the right shows the playing field, where two teams simultaneously compete. The blocks in the middle (blue) platform are availible to both sides and are worth more, thus offering an advantage to whichever side can claim them first. The stepped platforms on each end of the field serve as the goal areas for each side, where placing blocks on higher steps corresponds to more points. The central rail also offers the option for teams to build rail-mounted robots. The original contest webpage, which includes detailed rules and specifications, can be found here.
