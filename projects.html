MAE3 Robot Design

This robot was a team project for the robot contest in MAE3 (Introduction to Engineering Graphics and Design). The goal was to build a user-controlled robot that could transport blocks from various locations on the playing field and deposit them in a designated area to score points.

MAE150 Cam Design

The goal of this project was to design a pair of cams for a laser "lightshow" setup. A laser rests on the two cams, and thus its position is dependent on the varying radii of the cams. A desired path for the laser was first specified, then the cams were created based on this desired laser path.
